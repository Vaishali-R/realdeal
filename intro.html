My name's Vaishali. I just finished my degree and I am still unemployed. Everyone keeps asking me why?? am I still not employed and quite frankly I have no answer.
So I continue doing what I do best, that is, control the urge to punch them in the face and carry on with "my work".

Ironically, "my work" pretty much involves just sitting in front of my laptop (my love) and learning new stuff.
It's like a journey for me, discovering new things each day (wherever the hell they were all this time), brainstorming about what to do with what I've learnt
and then going to bed with the satisfaction of having learnt something amazing, new and useful.

That's pretty much how I've been spending my free time and believe it or not, this period makes up for all the four freaking years
I spent fooling myself into thinking that I gained some "knowledge".

Since I officially have no job, I thought I'd make a website telling people how I officially don't have a job and what I spend my free time doing.
Now, this whole website-building business is something of a new skill I recently acquired from a really awesome website, I call it "The Webdev Hamlet".
I highly recommend it, if you're upto no good either.
So to show the world my usefulness, I have put together all the things I learnt in the Hamlet. And also some of my passtime stuffs. Please visit all the pages and try to enjoy.
